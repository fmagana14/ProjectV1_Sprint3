Our founding

Sip Sum was founded to revolutionize hydration with a commitment to sustainability, crafting durable and stylish metal water bottles that offer an eco-friendly alternative to single-use plastics. The mission is to inspire a positive impact on the environment while providing individuals with high-quality, reusable companions for their daily hydration needs.

Growth & beyond

At Sip Sum, we actively contribute to environmental change by promoting the use of reusable metal water bottles, significantly reducing the reliance on single-use plastics. Through our commitment to eco-friendly practices and sustainable production, we strive to inspire a broader shift towards responsible consumption and a healthier planet.
